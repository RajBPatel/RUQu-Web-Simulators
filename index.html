◆ RUQu: Physics & Quantum Simulators

A collection of interactive calculators and simulators for optics, quantum mechanics, and error propagation.

📏

OPL & Time Calculator

Calculate Optical Path Length and Time of Flight for ultrafast setups dynamically using varying fibers, waveplates, and lenses.

🔦

Gaussian Beam Simulator

Interactive 2D propagation simulator with lenses, mirrors, dielectric slabs, and waist-fitting algorithms.

🎯

Fiber Coupling Optimizer

Optimize coupling efficiency into various optical fibers using idealized or thick lenses with auto-coupling algorithms.

🌐

Poincaré Sphere Simulator

Visualize polarization states and waveplate transformations (QWP, HWP) in real-time on a 3D Poincaré sphere.

📊

Photon Number Distribution

Calculate and visualize the probability distribution of detecting $n$ photons for Coherent, Thermal, and Squeezed states.

📉

Attenuated Photon Calculator

Calculate attenuated mean photon numbers, effective peak power, and visualize quantum distributions based on added dB attenuation.

🌊

PPKTP Walkoff & HOM

Simulate Type-II SPDC featuring Symmetric Group Velocity Matching, Joint Spectral Intensity, and Hong-Ou-Mandel dips.

⚡

Pulse & Power Calculator

Explore Time-Bandwidth products and calculate peak power, intensity, and fluence for various laser pulse shapes.

🧮

Error Propagation

Symbolically calculate partial derivatives and apply the general error propagation formula directly from LaTeX inputs.

🔔

Fock State Heralding

Analyze bi-directional heralding efficiencies and conditional state collapse across asymmetric channels.
